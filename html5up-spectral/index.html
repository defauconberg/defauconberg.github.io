Bio

As a PhD Candidate in the Organisational Theory & Information Systems Group at Cambridge Judge Business School, I focus on qualitative and mixed-methods research specializing in identity, innovation, and climate change mitigation. My current work seeks to understand the challenges that large organizations – particularly active fossil fuel producers – face as they pursue alternative energy-related innovation. I am fully supported in my research as a Gates Cambridge Scholar.
